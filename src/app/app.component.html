Account Deletion Guide

This guide will walk you through deleting your account from the Clubair mobile application.

Before We Begin:

  • Deleting your account is permanent.
  • Any account details will be removed immidiately.
  • Any account details will be removed from any ticket you purchased.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open Clubair mobile app on your device.
  2. Open menu from home screen.
  3. Open the settings screen from the settings option in the opened menu.
  4. Click on 'Remove Account' button from settings screen.
  5. If you are sure about your account removal click the yes button on the pop up.
  6. Dont close the application until the process is completed. Once it is completed you will be navigated to app login screen
